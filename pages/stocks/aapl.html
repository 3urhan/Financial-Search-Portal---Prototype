Overview

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Apple is the world's largest technology company by revenue, and one of the most valuable companies in the world.

The company designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Its hardware products include the iPhone smartphone, iPad tablet computer, Mac personal computer, Apple Watch smartwatch, and Apple TV digital media player.
