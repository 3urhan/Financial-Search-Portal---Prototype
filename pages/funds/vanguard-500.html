Overview

The Vanguard 500 Index Fund seeks to track the performance of the S&P 500 Index, which measures the investment return of large-capitalization stocks. The fund employs an indexing investment approach designed to track the performance of the S&P 500 Index.

Portfolio

The fund invests in all 500 stocks in the S&P 500 Index in approximately the same proportions as the index itself. Top holdings include major technology companies, financial institutions, and healthcare firms.
